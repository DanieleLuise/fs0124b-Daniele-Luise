Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions,tart
cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpouse flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspons salt
  • 1 1/3 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/54 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, tought it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix together al
the dought ingridients until the flour is full absorbed. You dont'need to actually knead the
dougt, thought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporated
the last on the flour...
